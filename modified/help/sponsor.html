Sponsorship Options

There are many ways to support Thrangu Ko Jong Sherab Ling Nunnery. Besides supporting the new medical center project, one may wish to contribute to the daily costs of running the nunnery, or support a nun in three-year-retreat. What funding is needed at the Nunnery?

Daily Necessities:

1. Food – Nuns at the monastery are nourished by the generosity of the townspeople. However, the influx of converting nuns to the monastery is creating a shortage of food. Currently over 100 nuns are relying on butter, cheese, and the slim donations they receive through begging.
2. Lamps, especially during pujas
3. Supplies for the kitchen and library
4. Blankets
5. Monetary donations to cover the costs of heat, electricity, medicine, teaching material, and transportation

Itemized list of weekly expenses:

$25 – 5 lbs of butter
$25 – 3 lbs of Yak Dung used for heating
